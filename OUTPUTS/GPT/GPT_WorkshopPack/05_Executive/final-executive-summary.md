## 1. Workshop purpose _(Day 1 — provisional)_
To create shared understanding of the Web Transformation programme (migrate to a new Contentstack-based platform, uplift CX, decommission legacy), integrate the delivery teams, and surface deliverables, dependencies, risks and constraints to consolidate a programme plan ahead of a fixed end-November delivery.

## 2. Strategic context _(Day 1 — provisional)_
The programme spans business (audience simplification, commercialisation/multi-tenant ambition), technical (Contentstack, V2 templates, OMDS, environments, observability, automated testing), process (publishing workflow, content governance, handover gates) and governance (design authority, approvals, go-live readiness). The central strategic question is whether this is a migration, a transformation, or a bounded hybrid.

## 3. Key outcomes _(Day 1 — provisional)_

- Identified capacity (esp. single-person publishing) and the absence of a centralised knowledge system as binding constraints.
