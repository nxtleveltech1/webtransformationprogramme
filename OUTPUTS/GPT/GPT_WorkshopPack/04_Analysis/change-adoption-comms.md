# G. Change, Adoption & Communications

> **GPT review stamp:** GPT-reviewed artefact | 2026-06-03 | Day 1 provisional | Day 2 pending unless explicitly evidenced. This file is a reviewed copy of the WorkshopPack baseline; uncertainty remains explicit and no Day 2 outcomes are invented.


**Source:** SRC-001, SRC-002 (Day 1).

## Impacted users
- Customers across migrated country/business sites and secure-web journeys (mix of old/new designs during transition).
- Internal teams: publishers, designers, BAs, regional teams, service team, call centre.
- Business units submitting design requests (currently unstructured except OMF).

## Behaviour / ways-of-working changes
- Move to structured design briefs using platform components (OMF is the model; others must adopt).
- Adopt entry/exit gates and handover criteria between teams.
- Adopt single shared knowledge system (currently missing → delays).
- Adopt OMDS governance (component lifecycle, approval SLAs, exception process).

## Training / documentation needs
- Onboarding for new team members needs clear, actionable deliverables (not strategic input).
- Documentation of journeys via journey maps so journeys aren't lost in migration.
- Documented & approved design/template approach.
- Content standards documented inside the design system.

## Adoption risks / resistance points
- Business units resisting standardised templates → mitigated by offering template variants (buy-in).
- Non-migrated sites drifting from OMDS without controlled adoption model (RSK-012).
- Cluster/SME engagement gaps blocking content (RSK-009/016).
- Capacity strain reducing quality if not managed (RSK-002).

## Communications required
- Call-centre + internal communications for go-live (ISS-005).
- E-commerce comms before EasiPlus changes (marketing spend impact).
- **Notify e-commerce (Nthabi) of URL changes** — ~95% of URLs changing affects GA tracking + exec sales/traffic reporting (ACT-046, RSK-021).
- **SEO engagement-frequency requirements communicated to the CFO** (ACT-012).
- **Faoli Bank** distinct migration/branding requirements communicated to relevant teams (DEC-013).
- Change-expectation comms for non-migrated sites (what changes now / stays as-is / moves later).
- Rollout/demo approach to showcase target experience, validate enhanced components, manage expectations.

## Knowledge & documentation (S2/S3)
- Stand up a **centralised documentation/knowledge system** (processes, history, relationships) — root-cause fix for delays (ACT-054, ISS-008, RSK-020).
- Hold an alignment session on design-system taxonomy (compositions/components/patterns/templates + naming) (ACT-048).

## Business readiness / operational handover
- Go-live readiness checklist (PGW testing, QAG, ServiceNow, security, comms, decommission) — ACT-013.
- Operational handover incl. support model for migrated sites.
- Maintain existing design estate during transition to avoid inconsistency.
