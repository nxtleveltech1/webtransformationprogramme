The workshop brought together cross-functional teams to establish clear delivery plans, dependencies, and governance structures for a program that must be completed by end of 2026 due to funding constraints.

Key Challenge: The program has only 5 months remaining (June-November) due to freeze periods in December, with significant scope including multiple country sites, complex stakeholder dependencies, and substantial technical and design work.
