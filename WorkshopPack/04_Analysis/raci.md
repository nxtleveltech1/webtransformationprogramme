## B2. Gatekeeper accountability (DEC-019) — explicit

- **Accountable for go-live quality gate:** the **design & publishing teams act as gatekeepers**. Every page (including tenant/country pages) is reviewed and signed off **before** it goes live; pages not meeting standards are **not published**.
- **Named gatekeepers:** Natalie and Bernice (publishing), with Gareth holding template/standards authority. Only **Gareth and Natalie currently build templates** — any new team/tenant must be **trained to the standards before publishing** (onboarding is a precondition to contributing — ACT-067).
- **Consequence / risk:** this concentrates accountability in very few people → single-point-of-failure (RSK-026) and governance/template-drift risk if controls slip (RSK-027). Escalate capacity + onboarding.
