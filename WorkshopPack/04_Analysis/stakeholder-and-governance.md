# Day 2 re-baseline / update (SRC-008 D2S1, SRC-009 D2S2)

> Day 2 firmed the governance model around **template sign-off**, a **publishing gatekeeper gate**, and the **tenant funding/operating model**, and reaffirmed **bank ownership**. The Day 1 stakeholders/sign-offs above remain; the items below add the Day 2 governance baseline.

## F1. Template sign-off approvals & acceleration (QST-025, RSK-028, ACT-065)

- Templates are grouped **by page type and stakeholder group** to streamline sign-off and **reduce repetitive approvals**; the first batch limits changes to intrinsic design components.
- **Acceleration:** clarify the approval process and **accelerate sign-off** for category and product landing templates **before end-June** (Gareth/Bernice); sequential per-segment outreach (esp. Personal) risks extending timelines materially (RSK-028).
- **Open:** *what specific approvals* are required for template sign-off, and how to accelerate them, is still open (QST-025).
- Stakeholders organised into **clusters per audience** (personal, corporate, wealth, business) to align audience groupings, sign off IA, and collaborate on content audit (delete/amend/create) incl. **legal/compliance**.

## F2. Gatekeeper governance (DEC-019) — CONFIRMED

- **Design & publishing teams are gatekeepers:** every page reviewed and **signed off before go-live**; pages not meeting standards are **not published**.
- The team acts as **platform gatekeeper** — all tenants/users of the template framework must follow the sign-off process before content goes live.
- Only **Gareth & Natalie** build templates; new teams/tenants must be **trained to standards before publishing** (onboarding precondition — ACT-067). Firms Day 1 DEC-007.
- **Governance risk:** template/governance **drift** if controls slip, especially Contentstack ↔ Figma and across new tenants (RSK-027); SPOF concentration (RSK-026).

## F3. Tenant funding / operating model & formal agreements (DEC-026, QST-021, ACT-080)

- Post-migration, **tenants fund and manage their own sites** under **formal partnership agreements** (business owner + funding model); ongoing optimisation becomes a partnership.
- **Status: Proposed** — ownership models and formal agreements to be defined (Gareth/Reza — ACT-080); unclear assets (fund tables, Bulletin) need owners + working model (ACT-081, PRK-011).
- Migrated sites require **formal agreements to clarify business ownership and future funding**, which influence ongoing optimisation effort.

## F4. Bank ownership (DEC-028 / DEC-013)

- The **bank builds its own web pages**; the team is involved **only on a bank-initiated redesign** (no current timeline set). Reinforces Faoli Bank separate-brand treatment (DEC-013).
- **Open:** confirm whether **OM finance pages** currently managed by the team **move to the bank** (QST-021, ACT-076).

## F5. Leadership sign-off & cadence additions
- Leadership **final review and sign-off** required for cross-version/cross-section outputs before rollout.
- Governance cadence now defined: **twice-weekly 30-min leadership check-ins** + **weekly playback** (DEC-027) — see delivery-and-programme.md.
